Why studying in Germany is the right choice for you

Reduced Fees

And sometimes nothing at all! Students usually do not have to pay tuition fees, and if they do, these fees are small: bachelor's programs are often tuition-free at public universities, some master's programs have tuition fees, but these are not as high as in other countries.

University Life

Around 12 percent of the students at German universities come from abroad. Like you, you can make friends from all over the world and thus get to know other countries and broaden your horizons. Universities offer you support to make your start in Germany as easy as possible.

Acceptable Cost

In a European comparison, the cost of living in Germany is very cheap: the costs for food, rent, clothing and cultural activities are on EU level, and there are also a number of perks for students. Where they can get reduced prices in theatres, museums, operas, cinemas, swimming pools and other establishments.
